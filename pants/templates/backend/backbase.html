This is your reference pant, and all your search returns are described in relation to it.

This pant is designed to sit at your natural waist, which means that the waistband should be around the widest part of your body without cupping your crotch too tightly. It's also designed to be about the same level of tightness along your leg from your hip to your knee, neither particularly "relaxed" nor "slim fitting". Whether that's true or not depends on your body though.

The cuff and the knee are the same dimensions, so the pant will create a straight line from the knee to the ankle.
